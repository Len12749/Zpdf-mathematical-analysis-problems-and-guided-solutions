## 题干

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e - (1 + x)^{\frac{1}{x}}}{x}
$$

### 证明

分子变形后使用等价无穷小替换及 Taylor 展开：

$$
\lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e - e^{\frac{\ln(1 + x)}{x}}}{x} = \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{e \left(1 - e^{\frac{\ln(1 + x)}{x} - 1}\right)}{x} \newline
$$

$$
= -e \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\frac{\ln(1 + x)}{x} - 1}{x} = -e \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{\ln(1 + x) - x}{x^2} = -e \lim_{x \to 0} \frac{x - \frac{x^2}{2} + o(x^2) - x}{x^2}
$$

显然结果为：
$$
-e \cdot \left(-\frac{1}{2}\right) = \frac{e}{2}
$$
