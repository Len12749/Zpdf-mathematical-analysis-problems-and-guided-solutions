## 题干

若$z=f(\mu,\nu)+\varphi(\nu)$，$f,\varphi$可微，$x=\mu^2+\nu^2$，$y=\mu^3-\nu^3$，求$\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}$.

### 证明

首先，我们其进行初步求偏导计算：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial z}{\partial f}\cdot\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial z}{\partial \varphi}\cdot\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x}
$$

进一步地去求各个部分：

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial f}{\partial \mu}\cdot\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial \nu}\cdot\frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x} \newline
\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \nu}\cdot\frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x}
\end{gathered}
$$

由$x=\mu^2+\nu^2,y=\mu^3-\nu^3$，两边对$x$求偏导：

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\partial x}{\partial x}=1=2\mu\cdot\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x}+2\nu\cdot\frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x} \newline
\frac{\partial y}{\partial x}=0=3\mu^2\cdot\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x}-3\nu^2\cdot\frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x}
\end{gathered}
$$

解得：

$$
\frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x}=\frac{\nu}{2\mu(\nu-\mu)},\quad \frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x}=\frac{\mu}{2\nu(\mu-\nu)}
$$

从而：

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\partial f}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial f}{\partial \mu}\cdot\frac{\nu}{2\mu(\nu-\mu)}+\frac{\partial f}{\partial \nu}\cdot\frac{\mu}{2\nu(\mu-\nu)} \newline
\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial x}=\frac{\partial \varphi}{\partial \nu}\cdot\frac{\mu}{2\nu(\mu-\nu)}
\end{gathered}
$$

则：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x}=f_\mu\cdot\frac{\nu}{2\mu(\nu-\mu)} + \left(f_\nu + \varphi'(\nu)\right)\cdot\frac{\mu}{2\nu(\mu-\nu)}
$$
