## 题干

$$
\lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\frac{n+2}{n+1}\right)^n
$$

### 证明

取对数后使用等价无穷小替换：

$$
\lim_{n\to\infty} n\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n+1}\right) = \lim_{n\to\infty} \frac{n}{n+1} = 1
$$

由连续性可得：

$$
\lim_{n\to\infty}\left(\frac{n+2}{n+1}\right)^n = \lim_{n\to\infty}e^{n\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n+1}\right)} = e^{\lim_{n\to\infty} n\ln\left(1+\frac{1}{n+1}\right)} = e^1 = e
$$
