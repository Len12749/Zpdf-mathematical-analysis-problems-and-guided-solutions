# 194

## 题干

已知$x_1=2$，$x_{n+1}=2+\frac{1}{x_n}$.

试证明其奇数子列和偶数子列的单调性.

### 证明

不妨先写出几项寻找规律：

$$
\begin{gathered}
x_1=2,\quad x_2=2+\frac{1}{2}=\frac{5}{2},\quad x_3=2+\frac{2}{5}=\frac{12}{5},\newline
x_4=2+\frac{5}{12}=\frac{29}{12},\quad x_5=2+\frac{12}{29}=\frac{70}{29},\quad x_6=2+\frac{29}{70}=\frac{169}{70}
\end{gathered}
$$

故显然：

$$
\begin{gathered}
x_1<x_3<x_5<x_6<x_4<x_2\newline
2<2.4<2.413<2.414<2.416<2.5
\end{gathered}
$$

假设归纳，已知：

$$
x_1<x_3<\cdots<x_{2k-1}<x_{2k}<x_{2k-2}<x_{2k-4}<\cdots<x_4<x_2
$$

则试图证明：

$$
x_1<x_3<\cdots<x_{2k-1}<x_{2k+1}<x_{2k+2}<x_{2k}<x_{2k-2}<x_{2k-4}<\cdots<x_4<x_2
$$

只需要证明：

$$
x_{2k-1}<x_{2k+1}<x_{2k+2}<x_{2k}
$$

易见：

$$
\begin{gathered}
x_{2k-1}=2+\frac{1}{x_{2k-2}}<2+\frac{1}{x_{2k}}=x_{2k+1} \quad (\because x_{2k-2} > x_{2k})\newline
x_{2k+1}=2+\frac{1}{x_{2k}}<2+\frac{1}{x_{2k+1}}=x_{2k+2} \quad (\because x_{2k} > x_{2k+1})\newline
x_{2k+2}=2+\frac{1}{x_{2k+1}}<2+\frac{1}{x_{2k-1}}=x_{2k} \quad (\because x_{2k+1} > x_{2k-1})
\end{gathered}
$$

由归纳法得证该规律恒成立，因此：

奇数子列$\{x_{2k-1}\}$严格单调递增.

偶数子列$\{x_{2k}\}$严格单调递减.
