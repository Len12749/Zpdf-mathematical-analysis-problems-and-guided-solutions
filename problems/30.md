## 题干

设$z = f(\xi, \eta)$，其中$\xi = r, \eta = r \cos \theta$，求：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial r}, \frac{\partial z}{\partial \theta}
$$

### 证明

使用复合函数求偏导（链式法则）可得：

$$
\begin{gathered}
\frac{\partial z}{\partial r} = f_{\xi} + \cos\theta \, f_{\eta} \newline
\frac{\partial z}{\partial \theta} = f_{\eta} \cdot (-r \sin\theta) = -r \sin\theta \, f_{\eta}
\end{gathered}
$$
