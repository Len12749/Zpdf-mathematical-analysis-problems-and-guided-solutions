## 题干

$z=f(xy,x+y)$，$f$二阶连续可微，求$\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y}$和$\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial y^2}$。



### 证明

设$\mu=xy,\nu=x+y$.

则：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial x} = \frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu} \cdot \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial x} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial \nu} \cdot \frac{\partial \nu}{\partial x} = y \frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial \nu}
$$

那么：

$$
\frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial x \partial y} = \frac{\partial}{\partial y} \bigl( y \frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial \nu} \bigr)
$$

即：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu} + y \left( \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \mu^2} \cdot \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \mu \partial \nu} \cdot \frac{\partial \nu}{\partial y} \right) + \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \nu \partial \mu} \cdot \frac{\partial \mu}{\partial y} + \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \nu^2} \cdot \frac{\partial \nu}{\partial y}
$$

代入：

$$
f_1 + xy f_{11} + (x + y) f_{12} + f_{22}
$$

同样地：

$$
\frac{\partial z}{\partial y} = x \frac{\partial z}{\partial \mu} + \frac{\partial z}{\partial \nu}
$$

继续对$y$求偏导：

$$
x \left( x \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \mu^2} + \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \mu \partial \nu} \right) + x \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \nu \partial \mu} + \frac{\partial^2 z}{\partial \nu^2}
$$

代入：

$$
x^2 f_{11} + 2 x f_{12} + f_{22}
$$
