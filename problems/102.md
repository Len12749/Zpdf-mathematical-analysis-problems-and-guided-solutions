## 题干

$z=f(xy,x+y)$，$f$二阶连续可微，求$\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{x}\part{y}}$和$\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{y^2}}$.



### 证明

设$\mu=xy,\nu=x+y$.

则：
$$
\frac{\part{z}}{\part{x}}=\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\mu}}\cdot\frac{\part{\mu}}{\part{x}}+\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\nu}}\cdot\frac{\part{\nu}}{\part{x}}=y\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\mu}}+\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\nu}}
$$
那么：
$$
\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{x\part{y}}}=\frac{\part}{\part{y}}(y\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\mu}}+\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\nu}})
$$
即：
$$
\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\mu}}+y(\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\mu^2}}\cdot\frac{\part{\mu}}{\part{y}}+\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\mu}\part{\nu}}\cdot\frac{\part{\nu}}{\part{y}})+\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\nu}\part{\mu}}\cdot\frac{\part{\mu}}{\part{y}}+\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\nu^2}}\cdot\frac{\part{\nu}}{\part{y}}
$$
代入：
$$
f_1+xyf_{11}+(x+y)f_{12}+f_{22}
$$
同样地：
$$
\frac{\part{z}}{\part{y}}=x\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\mu}}+\frac{\part{z}}{\part{\nu}}
$$
继续对$y$求偏导：
$$
x(x\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\mu}^2}+\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\mu}\part{\nu}})+x\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\nu}\part{\mu}}+\frac{\part^2{z}}{\part{\nu}^2}
$$
代入：
$$
x^2f_{11}+2xf_{12}+f_{22}
$$
