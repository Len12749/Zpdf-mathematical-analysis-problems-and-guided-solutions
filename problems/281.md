## 题干

设$x_n=\int_0^1 x^2(1-x)^ndx$，求$\sum_{n=1}^\infty x_n$.



### 证明

$$
x_n=\int_0^1x^2(1-x)^ndx
$$

设$t=1-x,x=1-t,t\in(1,0)$，区间再现：
$$
x_n=\int_0^1(1-t)^2t^ndt=\frac{1}{n+1}-\frac{2}{n+2}+\frac{1}{n+3}
$$
显然可递推：
$$
\begin{gathered}
(\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{3}+\frac{1}{4})+(\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{4}+\frac{1}{5})+(\frac{1}{4}-\frac{1}{5}-\frac{1}{5}+\frac{1}{6})+\cdots\newline+(\frac{1}{n+1}-\frac{1}{n+2}-\frac{1}{n+2}+\frac{1}{n+3})
\end{gathered}
$$
归纳可得：
$$
\frac{1}{2}-\frac{1}{3}-\frac{1}{n+2}+\frac{1}{n+3}\to\frac{1}{6}
$$
