## 题干

试证明：二元函数全微分中的$o(\rho)$与$\alpha\Delta x+\beta\Delta y$是等价的.

其中：$\rho=\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}$；$\alpha$和$\beta$都是$(\Delta x,\Delta y)$在$(0,0)$处的无穷小量.



### 证明

${\Rightarrow}$

由于全微分存在：

$$
\Delta z=f(x+\Delta x,y+\Delta y)-f(x,y)=A\Delta x+B\Delta y+R(\Delta x,\Delta y),\\R(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\in o(\rho),\rho=\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}
$$
于是可以取$\alpha=\frac{R\Delta x}{\rho^2}$，$\beta=\frac{R\Delta y}{\rho^2}$；我们讨论极限时不会考虑$\rho=0$的情况，该式满足：
$$
\alpha\Delta x+\beta\Delta y=\frac{R(\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2)}{\rho^2}=\frac{R\rho^2}{\rho^2}=R=o(\rho)
$$
且$\alpha$满足：

$$
\lim\limits_{(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\rightarrow(0,0)}\alpha=\lim\limits_{(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\rightarrow(0,0)}(\frac{R}{\rho}\cdot\frac{\Delta x}{\rho})=\lim\limits_{(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\rightarrow(0,0)}(\frac{o(\rho)}{\rho}\cdot \frac{\Delta x}{\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}})
$$
容易看出，该极限式为**无穷小×有界量**.

即证出了：$\alpha=o(1)$，$(\Delta x,\Delta y)\rightarrow(0,0)$.

同理可证：$\beta=o(1)$，$(\Delta x,\Delta y)\rightarrow(0,0)$.

$\Leftarrow$

$$
\lim\limits_{(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\rightarrow(0,0)}\frac{\alpha\Delta{x}+\beta\Delta{y}}{\rho}=\lim\limits_{(\Delta{x},\Delta{y})\rightarrow(0,0)}(\alpha\cdot\frac{\Delta x}{\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}}+\beta\cdot\frac{\Delta y}{\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}})
$$
其中$\frac{\Delta x}{\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}}$和$\frac{\Delta y}{\sqrt{\Delta x^2+\Delta y^2}}$都是有界量.

故原极限为$0$，$\alpha \Delta x+\beta\Delta y=o(\rho)$.
