## 题干

设$f(x+h)=f(x)+f'(x)h+\frac{1}{2!}f''(x)h^2+\cdots+\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x+\theta h)h^n,(\theta\in(0,1))$.

且$f^{(n+1)}(x)\neq0$，证明：
$$
\lim\limits_{h\to0}\theta=\frac{1}{n+1}
$$



### 证明

由$Taylor$展开：
$$
\begin{gathered}
f(x+h)=f(x)+f'(x)h+\frac{1}{2!}f''(x)h^2+\cdots\newline+\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x)h^n+\frac{1}{(n+1)!}f^{(n+1)}(x)h^{n+1}+o(h^{n+1}),
(\theta\in(0,1))
\end{gathered}
$$
可得：
$$
\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x+\theta h)h^n=\frac{1}{n!}f^{(n)}(x)h^n+\frac{1}{(n+1)!}f^{(n+1)}(x)h^{n+1}+o(h^{n+1})
$$
同除$h^{n+1}$：
$$
\frac{f^{(n)}(x+\theta h)-f^{(n)}(x)}{n!h}=\frac{f^{(n+1)}(x)}{(n+1)!}+o(1)
$$
进一步：
$$
\frac{f^{n}(x+\theta h)-f^{(n)}(x)}{\theta h}\cdot\theta=\frac{f^{(n+1)}(x)}{n+1}+o(1)
$$
整理为：
$$
\lim\limits_{h\to0}\theta=\lim\limits_{h\to0}\frac{\frac{f^{(n+1)}(x)}{n+1}+o(1)}{\frac{f^{n}(x+\theta h)-f^{(n)}(x)}{\theta h}}=\frac{f^{(n+1)}(x)}{f^{(n+1)}(x)}\cdot \frac{1}{n+1}=\frac{1}{n+1}
$$
