## 题干

证明三角函数求和公式.

化简：
$$
\begin{gathered}
S_1=\mathop\Sigma\limits_{k=1}^{n}\sin{(kx)}=\sin{x}+\sin{(2x)}+\sin{(3x)}+\cdots+\sin{(nx)}\\
S_2=\mathop\Sigma_{k=1}^{n}\cos(kx)
\end{gathered}
$$


### 证明1

使用$\sin{A}\cdot\sin{B}=-\frac{1}{2}(\cos{(A+B)}-\cos{(A-B)})$：
$$
\begin{align}
S_1&=\frac{1}{\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\sin{\frac{x}{2}}\sin{x}+\sin{\frac{x}{2}}\sin{(2x)}+\sin{\frac{x}{2}}\sin{(3x)}+\cdots+\sin{\frac{x}{2}}\sin{(nx)})\\
&=\frac{1}{-2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\cos{\frac{3x}{2}}-\cos{\frac{x}{2}}+\cos{\frac{5x}{2}-\cos{\frac{3x}{2}}+\cdots+\cos{\frac{(2n+1)x}{2}}}\\&~~~~-\cos{\frac{(2n-1)x}{2}})\\
&=-\frac{1}{2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\cos{\frac{(2n+1)x}{2}-\cos{\frac{x}{2}}})\\
&=-\frac{1}{2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}\cdot(-2)\cdot\sin{\frac{(n+1)x}{2}}\cdot\sin{\frac{nx}{2}}\\
&=\frac{\sin{\frac{(n+1)x}{2}\sin{\frac{nx}{2}}}}{\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}
\end{align}
$$

### 证明2

使用：$\cos{A}\cdot\sin{B}=\frac{1}{2}(\sin{(A+B)}-\sin{(A-B)})$：
$$
\begin{align}
S=&\frac{1}{\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\cos{x}\sin{\frac{x}{2}}+\cos{(2x)}\sin\frac{x}{2}+\cos{(3x)\sin{\frac{x}{2}}+\cdots+\cos{(nx)\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}})\\
=&\frac{1}{2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\sin\frac{3x}{2}-\sin{\frac{x}{2}}+\sin{\frac{5x}{2}}-\sin\frac{3x}{2}+\cdots+\sin{\frac{(2n+1)x}{2}-\sin{\frac{(2n-1)x}{2}}})\\
=&\frac{1}{2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}(\sin{\frac{(2n+1)x}{2}}-\sin{\frac{x}{2}})\\
=&\frac{1}{2\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}\cdot2\cdot\cos{\frac{(n+1)x}{2}}\sin\frac{nx}{2}\\
=&\frac{\cos{\frac{(n+1)x}{2}}\sin{\frac{nx}{2}}}{\sin{\frac{x}{2}}}
\end{align}
$$
